Visa & Immigration

Essential visa and immigration details for travelers

Visa Requirements

Depending on your nationality and destination, you may require a visa. Below is general information about visa types and processing.

Transit Visa

Required for passengers transiting through the country. Processing time: 2-3 business days.

Tourist Visa

For leisure travel. Typically valid for 30-90 days. Processing time: 5-7 business days.

Business Visa

For business-related activities. Requires invitation letter. Processing time: 7-10 business days.
